# About the Interview Prep Resource

Welcome to the **Interview Prep Resource**! This project was created with a simple mission: to provide a centralized, high-quality, open-source repository of knowledge for Software Engineers preparing for rigorous technical interviews.

## What is this?
Preparing for software engineering interviews—especially at top-tier tech companies—can be incredibly overwhelming. The knowledge required spans across multiple domains:
- **Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA)**
- **High-Level System Design (HLD)**
- **Low-Level Design (LLD)**
- **Design Patterns**
- **Concurrency and Operating Systems**
- **Behavioral & Leadership Skills**

Often, resources for these topics are scattered across various books, paid courses, and fragmented blogs. This platform aims to bring everything under one roof, structured in an intuitive, easy-to-read format.

## Who is it for?
- **Entry-level Engineers** looking to build a strong foundation in core software principles.
- **Mid-level Engineers** preparing to transition to Senior roles by mastering System Design and Architecture.
- **Senior & Staff Engineers** brushing up on distributed systems, concurrency nuances, and leadership interviews.

## Our Philosophy
1. **Clarity over Complexity:** We believe complex engineering topics can be explained simply. We use real-world analogies, straightforward definitions, and clear examples.
2. **Visual Learning:** We heavily utilize Mermaid diagrams for architecture and flow charts, ensuring that you don't just read about a concept, but visualize it.
3. **Open and Free:** This resource is entirely open-source and free to use.

## The Journey
This project was initially started as a personal collection of notes. As the collection grew, it became clear that packaging this into a proper documentation site (powered by Next.js and Nextra) could benefit the wider developer community.

# Career Growth

## Introduction
Career growth is about setting a technical path, expanding influence, and developing leadership impact over time.

## Problem Statement
Many engineers struggle to grow because they focus only on individual tasks rather than broader ownership and leadership.

## Why this exists
A strong career journey combines technical depth, communication, mentorship, and business awareness.

## Real-world analogy
An apprentice becomes a master by practicing, learning from feedback, and mentoring others.

## Definition
Career growth is the process of developing skills, influence, and outcomes that match the next level of responsibility.

## Key concepts
- **Goal setting**
- **Mentorship**
- **Feedback culture**
- **Learning plan**
- **Cross-functional exposure**

## Best practices
- Define growth goals with your manager.
- Seek stretch opportunities and learn from failure.
- Mentor peers and share knowledge.

## Interview questions
### Beginner
- How do you set career goals?
- Answer: Identify strengths, areas to improve, and milestones for growth.

### Intermediate
- How do you ask for feedback?
- Answer: Be specific, ask for examples, and follow up on improvements.

### Senior
- How do you help a teammate grow?
- Answer: Provide mentorship, create learning opportunities, and recognize progress.
